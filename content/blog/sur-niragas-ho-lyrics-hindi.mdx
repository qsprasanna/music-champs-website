## About the Song

**Sur Niragas Ho** (सूर निरागस हो) is a deeply devotional song from the acclaimed Marathi film **Katyar Kaljat Ghusli** (2015). The song is a heartfelt prayer to **Lord Ganpati (Ganesha)** — the deity of beginnings and patron of the arts — asking that music remain pure and free from all impurity.

The meaning of _Sur Niragas Ho_ is: **"May the music be innocent and free from envy."**

- **Lyrics:** Mangesh Kangane
- **Vocals:** Shankar Mahadevan & Anandi Joshi
- **Music:** Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy

## About Katyar Kaljat Ghusli

The song is from the Marathi film **Katyar Kaljat Ghusli** (2015), which centres on the world of Indian classical music and the timeless rivalry between a guru and his student. The film is celebrated for its deeply authentic portrayal of Hindustani classical music, and the soundtrack became one of the most treasured in Indian film music.
